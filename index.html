Water

Cape Town, London, Sao Paolo, Jakarta, Istanbul, Tokyo, and Mexico City could be facing "Day Zero" — meaning they will run out of water — in the next few decades unless their water use radically changes. As global warming continues to take its tolls, the water crisis continues to grow. So one must ask themself, how much water is consumed in products we use everyday?

Hover over a circle to view the product and its consumption between 1996-2005. This data visualization shows animal products in blue and crop products in light blue.
